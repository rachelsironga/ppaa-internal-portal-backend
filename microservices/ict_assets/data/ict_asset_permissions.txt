## Permission Groups (Recommended)

### ICT Manager (Full Access)
- All add_* permissions
- All view_* permissions
- All change_* permissions
- All delete_* permissions
- bulk_update_asset
- bulk_delete_asset
- export_assets

### ICT Technician (Operational Access)
- add_asset, view_asset, change_asset
- add_computer, view_computer, change_computer
- add_networkdevice, view_networkdevice, change_networkdevice
- add_peripheral, view_peripheral, change_peripheral
- add_softwareinstallation, view_softwareinstallation, change_softwareinstallation
- add_maintenancerecord, view_maintenancerecord, change_maintenancerecord
- add_supportticket, view_supportticket, change_supportticket
- view_assetcategory, view_assettype, view_location, view_manufacturer, view_softwarecategory
- view_dashboard
- export_assets

### Department User (Limited Access)
- view_asset (own department only)
- view_computer (own department only)
- view_softwareinstallation (own assets only)
- view_supportticket (own tickets only)
- view_dashboard (limited view)

### Read-Only User (View Only)
- All view_* permissions
- view_dashboard
- export_assets

### Asset Auditor
- view_asset, view_computer, view_networkdevice, view_peripheral
- view_software, view_softwareinstallation
- view_maintenancerecord, view_warranty, view_disposalrecord
- export_assets
- view_dashboard

## Special Permission Notes

### Cross-Model Dependencies
- view_dashboard: Requires view_asset, view_maintenancerecord, and view_supportticket
- bulk_update_asset: Requires change_asset permission
- bulk_delete_asset: Requires delete_asset permission
- All lookup permissions require the corresponding view permission

### Data Sensitivity Levels
- HIGH SENSITIVITY: delete_*, disposal records, cost information
- MEDIUM SENSITIVITY: change_*, maintenance records, warranty details
- LOW SENSITIVITY: view_*, basic asset information

### Department-Based Permissions
- Department users should only see assets assigned to their department
- Technicians should see assets based on their assigned locations/buildings
- Managers have cross-department visibility
